Organiser Edition

Following on from Conflist, this is the Organisers Edition which is set for those who plan to or are putting on an event or conference. There's an awful lot more to think about when you're planning it!. Tick off the items you've done, pop your email address in the bottom and hit send, and you'll receive an email reminder telling you what you still need to sort out.

I hope this has helped you prepare for your fun conference visit. If you think I've left anything out, drop me a tweet @mrqwest and I'll add it to the list!

Oh and in case you're worried about privacy, we don't keep any details or email address' submitted through this form.
